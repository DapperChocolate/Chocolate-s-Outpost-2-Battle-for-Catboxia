# Chocolate-s-Outpost-2-Battle-for-Catboxia
GTAG VStump Map made for fun.

# Insallation

## PCVR

1.) Grab the release ZIP of your choice, then drag into your VStump maps folder.
2.) Subscribe to the map with a mod.io account that is linked to your Steam/GT account.

## Standalone

1.) Visit the mod.io website and subscribe to the map with an account that is linked to your Oculus/GT account.
